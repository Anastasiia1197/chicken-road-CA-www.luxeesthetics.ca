What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They serve as digital markers that help our platform recognize your browser and remember certain information about your preferences and activities. These files are widely used across the internet to make websites work more efficiently and provide a better user experience.

For Canadian players visiting Chicken Road, cookies enable seamless gameplay, save your progress, and ensure that our platform functions optimally on your device. They do not contain viruses or malware and cannot access other files on your computer. Cookies simply store information related to your interaction with our website, making each visit smoother and more personalized.
