Last Updated: October 28, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how www.luxeesthetics.ca (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information of visitors and players in Canada. It applies to all interactions with our website and related pages. By accessing or using the site, you acknowledge that this policy is tailored for Canadian users and follows core Canadian privacy principles of transparency, accountability, and purpose limitation.

We aim to provide a safe, fair, and compliant experience. Whether you browse guides, explore demo information, or interact with gameplay-related features, Chicken Road processes personal information only as reasonably necessary for service delivery and legal compliance in Canada.

Introduction

Our mission is to handle data responsibly for Canadian users. This policy describes what we collect, why we collect it, how we safeguard it, and the choices available to you. We retain information only for as long as needed to support the service, meet obligations, and prevent misuse or fraud.

  • Scope: this policy covers www.luxeesthetics.ca and pages under the Chicken Road brand.
  • Audience: players and visitors located in Canada, including prospective and returning users.

How We Use Your Information

We process data to deliver the service reliably and responsibly in Canada:

We apply administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information. While no method is entirely risk-free, we use layered controls aligned with industry practices to reduce the likelihood and impact of unauthorized access or loss.

If we become aware of a security incident affecting Canadian users, we will take prompt steps consistent with applicable notification standards.
